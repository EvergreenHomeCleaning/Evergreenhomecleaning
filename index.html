FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

What is your quality guarantee?

+
We stand behind every cleaning we perform. Before our team leaves your home, we always perform a final walkthrough with you. This is your opportunity to point out any concerns — we will address them on the spot at no extra cost. Important: once our team has left the property and the walkthrough has been completed, we cannot return for free re-cleans. All concerns must be raised during the walkthrough.

What's included in a Standard Cleaning?

+
Best for weekly or bi-weekly maintenance. Includes vacuuming all floors and carpets, mopping hard floors, full bathroom cleaning and sanitization (toilet, sink, shower, tub), kitchen countertops and sink cleaning, stovetop surface, dusting all accessible surfaces and furniture, wiping microwave exterior, cleaning and polishing all mirrors, and emptying trash bins with bag replacement.

What's included in a Deep Cleaning?

+
Best for first-time visits or homes not cleaned in 4+ weeks. Includes everything in Standard, PLUS: bathroom deep care (removing soap scum and hard water stains), kitchen degreasing (heavy-duty stovetop and burner cleaning), wiping exterior of all kitchen cabinets, deep cleaning the microwave interior, and vacuuming/mopping under accessible furniture. Note: baseboard wiping is available as a paid add-on.

What's included in a Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning?

+
Best for empty homes. Includes everything in Deep Cleaning, PLUS: detailed cleaning inside all kitchen and bathroom cabinets and drawers (included by default), wall spot cleaning (removing visible marks and fingerprints), and a full top-to-bottom reset of the entire empty property. Note: baseboard wiping is available as a paid add-on. Full wall washing or excessive stains may be subject to an extra charge.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

+
Yes! We bring all cleaning supplies and professional equipment. If you have specific products you prefer us to use, just let us know and we're happy to accommodate.

Are you licensed and insured?

+
Absolutely. Evergreen Home Cleaning is a fully licensed LLC in Washington state and carries liability insurance. Your home and belongings are protected while we work.

Who will be cleaning my home?

+
The owner personally handles every cleaning. No random crews or subcontractors — just one dedicated professional who knows your home and delivers consistent quality every visit.

What areas do you serve?

+
We're based in Bonney Lake, WA and serve the greater Pierce County and South King County areas, including Puyallup, Lake Tapps, Sumner, Auburn, Federal Way, Covington, Maple Valley, and surrounding communities.

What is your cancellation policy?

+
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling — this is always free. Late cancellations (less than 24 hours): $50 fee. Same-day cancellations: 50% of the booking total. No-show or lockout (we arrive and cannot access the home): 100% of the booking total.

How do recurring discounts work?

+
Recurring discounts apply to Standard Cleaning only: Weekly saves 15%, Bi-weekly saves 10%, and Monthly saves 5%. The discount is applied automatically to every visit. Use our price calculator above to see exact pricing for your home size.

When is payment due?

+
Payment is due immediately after the service is completed. For recurring clients, payment is due within 24 hours. We accept Cash, Zelle, or Debit Card. Please note: we do not accept credit cards.

Evergreen Home Cleaning LLC provides residential cleaning services in Pierce County and South King County, Washington. Our service types include:

Standard Cleaning — Regular maintenance cleaning. Includes vacuuming and mopping all floors, full bathroom sanitization (toilet, sink, shower, tub), kitchen countertops and stovetop surface, dusting all accessible surfaces and furniture, wiping microwave exterior, cleaning mirrors, and emptying trash bins.

Deep Cleaning — Everything in Standard, plus: removing soap scum and hard water stains, heavy-duty stovetop and burner cleaning, wiping exterior of all kitchen cabinets, deep cleaning microwave interior, and vacuuming/mopping under accessible furniture. Note: baseboard wiping is not included by default and is available as a paid add-on.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning — Everything in Deep Cleaning, plus: detailed cleaning inside all kitchen and bathroom cabinets and drawers, wall spot cleaning (visible marks and fingerprints), and a full top-to-bottom reset of the empty property. Interior cabinet cleaning is included by default. Baseboard wiping is available as a paid add-on. Full wall washing or excessive stains may be subject to additional charges.

Airbnb / Short-Term Rental Turnover — Optimized cleaning between guest stays. Includes kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, common areas, and floors. Laundry (linens and towels) is available as a paid add-on, priced by bed count.

The following are not included in any service type unless explicitly added as a paid add-on: moving personal belongings or boxes, cleaning surfaces or floors blocked by personal items, removing heavy set-in stains from walls/doors/carpets, window cleaning, baseboard wiping, laundry, dishes, organizing, cleaning under or behind heavy furniture, and exterior areas (garage, patio, balcony).
